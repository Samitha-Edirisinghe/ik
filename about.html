About Us

IK Lanka Engineering Solutions is well reputed leading Engineering Organization which is based on Mawanella Sri Lanka for Providing of Machines, Tools and Total Solution in Process Development solution systems for FMCG which includes both Foods and Non-Foods manufacturing related Industries. We are also provider of engineering service for designing and supplying custom machinery solutions according to requirement. We have serve huge variety of machines for manufacturing industries all around Sri Lanka. We have met exceeded industry standards while providing best Quality Machine and Reliable Service solutions to our clients.

We as IK Lanka Engineering assure that we supply best quality and durable user friendly machine solutions to our clients to develop cost-effective, productive, efficient and money saving solution which can fulfill their requirement.
