Double Cutting Pulverizie

Product Video

Description

Double Cutting Pulverizers is one of the new type of pulerizers, having two stages of grinding. In Double Cutting Pulverizer, the grinding material enters the first chamber and gets crushed to 75% of its fineness, later it enters the second chamber where the material is crushed more to obtain fine powder. The usage of double cutting pulverizer is quite common like that of any other machine. It comes very user friendly and both the genders can easily use these pulverizers without much trouble in handling. Single cutting pulverizers are usually free of maintenance. They do not require huge man power unlike other grinding machines. The fineness of the material can be achieved as the material passes a series of two chamber assembly. Double cutting pulverizers are most sought after by major industries and entrepreneurs.
